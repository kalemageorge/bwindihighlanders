3 Days of Mountain Rwenzori Hiking Safaris

Although most of the people thing that you need very many day to hike the mountain Rwenzori, it is important to note that you can as well do the hike in just three days. The 3 day mountain Rwenzori Hiking safari is the one of the very interesting hiking safari that will lead you to the mountain Ruwenzori dubbed the mountain of the moon. The Rwenzori mountains stenches high up to height of 5,109 meters above the sea level. For this great safari experience, you will either use the Kilemebe trekking route or the prominent Rwenzori central circuit to be able to complete this uphill adventure. Climbing mountain Ruwenzori is absolutely adventures and it can be booked for more than 3 days even going upto ten days to be able to react up to the snow caped mountain. In the south, the mountain is located not far from the equator line. In addition to guaranteeing your safety and security while traveling with us, we do organize trips to the area and can make one for you based on the days you have available. For your convenience, we have decide to come up with3 Day Mountain Rwenzori Hiking safari. The tour also includes lodging, camping equipment while on the mountain, and other services described within the Itinerary as listed below.

Day 1: Arrival to Entebbe International Airport- Meet and greet-Transfer Kasese town for overnight stay

You will be picked up from the airport or guest house after breakfast to begin your 3 day mountain Rwenzori Hiking safari. You can expect to spend about 8 hours driving. You will stop in Mubede for lunch before continuing on to Kasese to spend the night at a hotel nearby as you get ready for the following day's trek. You need to have enough rest for this hiking exercise.

Day 2; Kasese-Hike to Sine camp

On this day, you will move to the mountain's foothills to begin your ascent from 1,450 to Sine Hut 2,680m, where you spend the night. The Afro Montane Forest Zone's tall forest trees line the beginning of this hike, which continues with a steady climb through a variety of bird species and the potential to spot blue monkeys scampering through the forest. Visitors may occasionally catch a glimpse of groups of 15 to 20 black and white Colobus monkeys swinging through the trees. Rare sightings of the L'Hoest monkey, one of the cultural symbols of the Bukonzo people who are protected by the King or Omusinga, are possible. As you hike, don’t be surprised if you hear wild chimpanzee in the distance. From forest trees to bamboo thickets, low shrubs, flowers, fungi, and numerous moss and lichen covered vines hanging from the tall trees, the area is home to a wide variety of plant species. Only 200 meters away from Sine Hut, Enock's fall present a wonderful opportunity to create a wonderful screensaver for your memories. At Sine Camp, 2,596 meters above sea level, there are wooden huts tucked away between towering forest trees on a small ridge where you can sit and take in the splendor of the Afro Montane forest while gazing down at a magnificent waterfall tumbling over the rocks far below. In addition to the breathtaking views, those who are in shape and want to continue on to Kalalama Camp at 3,134 meters may do so as this gives you the extra time and additional experience at Mutinda Camp where you can ascend to Mutinda Lookout at 3,975 meters, aside from the wonderful views.

Day 3; Sine camp-Hike back to the foot of the mountain- transfer back to Kampala or Entebbe

Return back down the mountain after breakfast, then travel back to Kampala Entebbe to conclude your three-day mountain hiking tour in time for your departure flight.
